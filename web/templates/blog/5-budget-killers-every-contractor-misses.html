The Problem: You order 10% extra "just in case" but never track what actually gets used. That waste compounds across every job.

Real Example: Sarah's crew was consistently over-ordering drywall by 15%. Tracking revealed $240 waste per job, 12 jobs per year = $2,880 gone.
