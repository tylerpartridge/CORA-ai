Frequently Asked Questions

How does CORA calculate job profitability?

+

CORA calculates job profitability by comparing your estimated costs and revenue with actual costs and revenue. We track all expenses, labor costs, materials, and overhead, then provide real-time profit margins and alerts when projects are underperforming.

Can I import data from my existing accounting software?

+

Yes! CORA integrates with QuickBooks, Xero, and other popular accounting software. You can also import CSV files or connect your bank accounts directly through Plaid for automatic transaction syncing.

How secure is my financial data?

+

Your data is protected with bank-level encryption (AES-256), SOC 2 Type II compliance, and real-time security monitoring. We never share your data with third parties and provide complete audit trails for all account activity.

What if I need to cancel my subscription?

+

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. We'll export all your data for you, and you can continue accessing your account until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer phone support?

+

Yes! Premium and Enterprise customers get priority phone support. All customers can reach us via email, live chat, or schedule a video call with our construction business experts.

Can I use CORA on my mobile device?

+

Absolutely! CORA is fully responsive and works great on smartphones and tablets. You can track job progress, view reports, and manage your business from anywhere.
