About CORA

Built by contractors, for contractors. We're on a mission to help construction businesses thrive through better financial management.

Our Story

CORA was born from frustration. As construction business owners ourselves, we were tired of losing money on projects because we couldn't track costs in real-time. Spreadsheets were a nightmare, and existing software was either too complex or too expensive.

We knew there had to be a better way. So we built CORA - a simple, powerful tool that gives contractors the financial visibility they need to make profitable decisions. No more guessing. No more surprises.

Today, thousands of contractors across the country use CORA to track job profitability, manage cash flow, and grow their businesses with confidence.

Our Mission

Empowering Construction Businesses

We believe every construction business deserves the tools to succeed. Our mission is to provide contractors with the financial clarity and insights they need to make better decisions, avoid costly mistakes, and build profitable, sustainable businesses.
