Privacy Policy

Your data security is our foundation

WE DO NOT SELL YOUR DATA. Period. Your job data is yours.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Company name
  • Account creation date

Job & Financial Data

  • Expense descriptions and amounts
  • Job names and addresses
  • Vendor information
  • Receipt images (if uploaded)
  • Voice recordings (temporarily, for processing only)

Usage Data

  • Login times and frequency
  • Features used
  • Error logs
  • Device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide real-time job costing and profit tracking
  • Process voice commands for expense tracking
  • Generate financial reports and insights
  • Sync with QuickBooks (when authorized)
  • Send important service updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our AI categorization
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt algorithms
  • Database encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure cloud hosting with firewall protection
  • Two-factor authentication available
  • Voice recordings deleted after processing

4. Data Sharing

We NEVER sell your personal or business data. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted services like QuickBooks (only when you authorize)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of company sale or merger (with notice)
  • With Your Consent: Only when you explicitly agree

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can:

  • Export all your data at any time via CSV download
  • Delete your account and request complete data deletion
  • Update your information through account settings
  • Request data portability to another service

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in securely
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze service usage (anonymized)
  • Improve performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • QuickBooks: For accounting integration (optional)
  • Cloud Infrastructure: For secure hosting
  • Email Services: For transactional emails
  • Payment Processing: For subscription billing

Each service has their own privacy policy and security measures.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all your data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request complete account and data deletion
  • Export: Download your data in standard formats
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service

9. Children's Privacy

CORA is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored and processed in secure data centers in the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations, which maintain high security standards.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through the service. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@cora-app.com
Address: Built with pride in North America
Response Time: Within 48 business hours
