Privacy Policy

How CORA AI protects your data

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account creation date

Financial Data

  • Expense descriptions and amounts
  • Vendor information
  • Payment methods
  • Receipt images (if uploaded)

Usage Data

  • Login times and frequency
  • Features used
  • Error logs
  • Device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the CORA AI service
  • Process your expense data and generate insights
  • Send you important service updates and notifications
  • Improve our AI categorization algorithms
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure hosting on DigitalOcean with firewall protection

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (SendGrid for emails, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of company sale or merger (with notice)
  • With Your Consent: Only when you explicitly agree

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can:

  • Export your data at any time via CSV download
  • Delete your account and request data deletion
  • Update your information through your account settings

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze service usage
  • Improve our website performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • SendGrid: For sending emails
  • DigitalOcean: For hosting and infrastructure
  • Sentry: For error monitoring and debugging

Each service has their own privacy policy and security measures.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in CSV format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails

9. Children's Privacy

CORA AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored and processed in the United States and Canada. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through the service. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
